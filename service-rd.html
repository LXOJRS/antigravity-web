R&D, Consultancy & Workshops

This work starts when things are still unclear.

Context

Teams often sense that AI matters, but struggle to place it in their daily work. Questions are open, assumptions collide, and decisions feel premature. My role is to help create clarity early, before momentum turns into confusion.

Focus

I work with professionals across sectors to explore how AI influences thinking, decision-making, and creative work in practice. The focus is on interpretation, judgment, and responsibility, especially once machine output begins to shape human choices.

Format

Some projects take the form of research and experimentation. Others revolve around structured conversations that surface what is actually at stake. In workshops, teams develop a shared language that helps them work with AI more deliberately the next day.

Background

My background in film, rhetoric, and business strategy informs this approach. I pay close attention to how meaning is constructed, how language guides decisions, and how structure affects outcomes.

Perspective

Workshops, consultancy, and R&D are not separate tracks. They're different formats for the same kind of thinking.

I work in the space where uncertainty is still present, and direction hasn't fully formed yet.
