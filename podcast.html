AI-Rated Podcast

AI-Rated is where curiosity gets some room.

Concept

In this podcast, my girlfriend Morgan and I talk about AI the way it actually shows up in everyday life. Not as a roadmap or a manual, but as something that quietly reshapes culture, habits, expectations, and the way people talk about their work.

Tone

The tone is loose, but the questions are taken seriously.

Some episodes circle around online phenomena, synthetic media, or the latest AI oddity that suddenly feels normal. Others drift toward education, scams, or the strange social behaviors that appear once technology becomes persuasive enough. The starting point is rarely technical, and never instructional.

Goal

The goal is to stay interesting.

AI-Rated is meant to be accessible, sometimes entertaining, occasionally uncomfortable, and often reflective. We allow ourselves to follow side paths, change our minds mid-conversation, and sit with half-formed thoughts a little longer than usual.

Focus

We don’t try to keep up with AI news. We pay attention to how people respond to it.

That makes the podcast less about answers, and more about noticing patterns, language, and small shifts that usually pass unnoticed. The things that don’t look important yet, but probably are.

New episodes are released monthly, and can be found on YouTube and Spotify.
