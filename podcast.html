AI-Rated Podcast

AI-Rated is where curiosity gets some room.

Concept

In this podcast, my girlfriend Morgan and I talk about AI the way it actually shows up in everyday life. Not as a roadmap or a manual, but as something that quietly reshapes culture, habits, expectations, and the way people talk about their work.

Tone

The tone is loose, but the questions are taken seriously. Some episodes circle around online phenomena, synthetic media, or the latest AI oddity that suddenly feels normal.

Goal

The goal is to stay interesting. AI-Rated is meant to be accessible, sometimes entertaining, occasionally uncomfortable, and often reflective.
